this is who I am.

about me.

After years managing fast-paced craft cocktail bars, I finally gave up on struggling through dysfunctional point of sales systems. Instead of continuing to curse the designers who didn’t consult me when they made these products, I decided to become one of them.

Now I transform ideas into realities with user-centric thinking and a research-based design process.

I have a background in copywriting and experience managing humans and agile workflows. My true loves are grammar, combustion engines, the ocean and mezcal.

my process.

Research

I love interviewing users, watching how different people navigate devices, and programs, asking why. Every single person is different. In what ways do they overlap?

Understanding

Empathizing with users comes naturally to me after years in customer facing positions, but it grows from learning why and how they make decisions.

Strategy

I value spending the time and energy upfront to make a plan. I start every project with a view of the big picture and keep that in mind throughout.

Testing

The easiest way to validate functionality is to test it. The immediate value of watching a user interact with a product is fascinating to me.
